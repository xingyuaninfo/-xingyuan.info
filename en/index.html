About Us

XingYuan Tech focuses on providing professional remote talent staffing and HR administrative hosting services for various online platforms and digital enterprises.

We primarily offer talent recruitment, team staffing, personnel management, and operational coordination services, assisting partner companies in building efficient remote work teams based on their business needs. Unlike traditional physical office models, XingYuan Tech specializes in providing flexible remote work services to meet corporate expansion and operational efficiency requirements.

With Malaysian talent resources as our core, our services also cover talent recruitment and staffing in Southeast Asian countries such as Vietnam, Myanmar, and Thailand. By integrating a regional talent network with remote human resources management experience, XingYuan Tech provides partners with stable, flexible, and cost-effective HR solutions.

Core Services

Targeting online and cross-border business scenarios, XingYuan Tech focuses on providing remote work team recruitment, staffing, and basic hosting support to help enterprises build stable, high-matching, and cost-effective remote teams.

Based on your business needs, we provide technical talent for web development, system maintenance, and data analysis. Leveraging our precise screening mechanism, we help build highly compatible tech teams to meet your development goals.

Backend Moderation & Ops Teams

We supply talent for data entry, content moderation, backend processing, and administrative support, assisting enterprises in establishing stable backend teams for daily business processing and operational collaboration.
